Tree Gen

Instructions

    X
      Basic camera controls
    • Hold the left mouse button and move the mouse to rotate the camera around the tree.
    • Hold the right mouse button and move the mouse to pan the camera.
    • Use the scroll wheel to zoom in and out.
      Basic customization
    • You can change the colors of the tree, the leaves, the sky, the lighting, and the ground.
    • In addition you can change the tree's height and width, along with the number of branches.
    • Pressing "twist" will twist the tree a random amount and "straight" will straighten it up.
    • Twisting becomes a lot more apparent if you change the "lean" of the tree!
    • You can also press random to completely randomize the tree's settings!
    • Save the current view as an image by right clicking the screen and clicking "save image as..."
      Advanced customization
    • To customize further, you can go row by row, and branch by branch and adjust individual branch settings.
    • Branches have the same basic adjustable settings as the trunk, but with added X and Z rotation.
